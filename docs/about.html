About

Studio Reactive is an architectural design studio that combines creative vision with computational expertise to deliver exceptional built environments. We provide comprehensive architectural design services—from concept development and schematic design through construction documentation and fabrication coordination. Our work spans custom architectural elements, complex facades, sculptural installations, and signature interior features that define a space.

Our design approach is grounded in advanced parametric modeling and digital fabrication methodologies. Using tools like Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, Revit, and CATIA, we develop geometrically sophisticated designs that are buildable, cost-conscious, and constructable. We work directly with fabricators and contractors to ensure design intent translates seamlessly into physical reality—whether the project involves CNC-milled components, 3D-printed elements, or precision-manufactured assemblies.

Studio Reactive operates as a full-service design partner, offering visualization, rendering, and presentation support alongside core architectural services. We collaborate with architects, developers, and owners to realize ambitious design visions—providing the technical depth and execution capability to move projects from sketch to completion without compromise.

Services

  • Parametric systems and automation
  • Digital fabrication implementation
  • Interaction design and physical computing
  • Visualization and media production
